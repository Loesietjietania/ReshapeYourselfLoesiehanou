Payment Methods

Why RY Uses EFT Payments

At Reshape Yourself, we prioritize security, transparency, and value. That’s why we use EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer) as our primary payment method. EFT payments allow us to securely verify transactions before orders are processed, helping protect both our customers and our business.

By using EFT, we’re able to reduce processing costs and pass those savings on to you through competitive pricing and exclusive offers.

Payment Methods

Benefits of EFT Payments

Enhanced Security - EFT transactions take place directly between your bank and ours, reducing the risk of third-party fraud.

Better Value for You - By avoiding high credit card processing fees, we can pass those savings on to you through better pricing and exclusive offers.
